He had quite a number of sons, and they were all cool. But here are four official result of his troubled lovelife.

Odin's cool sons:

Thor is a hammer-wielding god associated with lightning, thunder, storms, sacred groves and trees, strength, the protection of humankind, hallowing, and fertility.

Baldur had the greatest ship ever built, Hringhorni, and there is no place more beautiful than his hall, Breidablik.

Vidar is a god among the Æsir associated with vengeance.

Váli is "the god of eternal light".
